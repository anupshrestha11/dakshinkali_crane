About us...

Dakshinkali crane services is owned and operated hirer of cranes with over twenty years of experience. We operate an extensive fleet of mobile cranes backed by a team of highly skilled and fully qualified supervisors,crane operators,riggers and support techinicians.

Our crane range in capacity from 1 ton to 50 ton mobile cranes. Our cranes feature the fully compliant to work on mining and construction sites throughtout kathmandu valley. Our operations are strategically located around the nepal with our office in kalanki to better serve the requirements of our customers.
